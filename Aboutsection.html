Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At Morning Star Pre-School & Child Care Center, we follow the Montessori approach to help children grow in all areas—learning, creativity, and respect for others. We celebrate different cultures by introducing children to traditions, languages, and customs from around the world. Through activities like art, music, and community events, we encourage self-expression and build strong connections with families. Our goal is to help children develop a love for learning and an appreciation for the world around them.

School Facilities

Our Montessori school offers a nurturing and stimulating environment designed to support the holistic development of every child. With state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure a safe, engaging, and enriching experience for all students.

School Bus

Our safe and reliable school buses ensure convenient transportation for students, with trained staff to provide a secure and comfortable journey to and from school.

Playground

Our spacious playground is equipped with age-appropriate play structures, encouraging physical activity, teamwork, and outdoor exploration in a safe environment.

Healthy Canteen

Our canteen serves nutritious and balanced meals, promoting healthy eating habits and providing students with the energy they need for an active day of learning.

Positive Learning

Our classrooms are designed to foster curiosity and independence, with Montessori materials and a positive learning atmosphere that encourages creativity and critical thinking.
